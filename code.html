Our Renaissance Legacy

Since 1542

Founded in Florence during the height of the Renaissance, our perfumery has been crafting exquisite fragrances using traditional methods passed down through generations of master perfumers.

Each bottle contains not just a fragrance, but a piece of history - meticulously recreated formulas from the Renaissance era, adapted for modern sensibilities.

Our Craftsmanship

Botanical Sourcing

We source the finest natural ingredients from around the world, just as Renaissance explorers brought back exotic botanics.

Traditional Methods

Using techniques documented in 16th century manuscripts, we extract essences with painstaking care.

Modern Precision

While honoring tradition, we apply contemporary quality standards to ensure consistency and safety.
